Level 1

Harishree Sessions

Intoduction to Malayalam writing
  • Learn how to write and read all Malayalam alphabets
  • Able to identify the sound and alphabet
  • Able to write small words

Level 2

Vaayanashaala Sessions

Fluency in Reading and Writing
  • Develop more familiarity with the language
  • Increase fluency in reading and writing
  • Expand your vocabulary
View More
Level 3

Thoolika Sessions

Foundation Course for Conversational and
Independent Malayalam writing
  • Understand the building blocks of the language to enable making sentences independently.
  • Usage of prepositions, plurals, common tenses and other usages needed for conversation
  • Practice simple conversations in Malayalam.

Kilikonchal Sessions
